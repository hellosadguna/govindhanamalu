🕉️ **108 Govinda Namalu – Tirumala Chanting Experience** 🙏

This is a beautifully crafted **devotional chanting app/web experience** designed for daily prayer, meditation, and spiritual connection. It presents the sacred **108 Govinda Namalu** in a unique **pair-wise Tirumala style**, making it easy to chant, understand, and remember.

✨ **Key Features:**

* 📿 **108 Govinda Namalu (Pair-wise)** – Traditional Tirumala chanting format
* 💡 **Simple Meanings** – Understand each divine name easily
* 🔔 **Govinda Hari Govinda Chorus** – Repeated after every pair for authentic bhajan flow
* 📱 **Mobile-First Design** – Optimized for smooth usage on phones
* 🛕 **Full-Screen Chant Mode** – Focus on one pair at a time (like Japam)
* 🔄 **Tap to Navigate** – Move forward/back with a simple tap
* 📿 **Auto Chant Mode** – Continuous chanting experience
* 🌙 **Spiritual UI** – Calm, temple-inspired design

🙏 Whether you are chanting during **morning prayers, temple visits, or meditation**, this app helps you stay connected with Lord Govinda in a peaceful and immersive way.
